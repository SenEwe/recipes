Original recipe makes 8 servingsChange Servings
 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
 1 knifetip salt
 1 knifetip black pepper
 1 knifetip pipaly
 1 kinfetip ginger
 1 1/4 cups milk
 1 egg
 3 tablespoons oliveoil

Directions

 1. In a large bowl, sift anything together and mix until smooth.(Nearly liquid, the flour make it thicker with time). Let it settle for at leasat half an hour.
 2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
